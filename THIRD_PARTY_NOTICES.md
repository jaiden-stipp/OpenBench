# Third-party software

RTLDeck distributions bundle unmodified platform-native tools from the YosysHQ OSS CAD Suite, including Icarus Verilog, Verilator, and Yosys. Those tools are separate works and retain their respective licenses and copyright notices.

The packaged OSS CAD Suite includes its license directory under `resources/oss-cad-suite/license`. Electron and JavaScript dependencies likewise retain their own licenses. The RTLDeck GPL-3.0-only license does not replace or relicense these third-party components.

Source distributions do not commit the OSS CAD Suite binaries. Maintainers download an official native release during the packaging workflow.
